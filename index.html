Why should you be interested in Interactive Media Major/Minor?

If you're interested in technology, design, and communication, Interactive Media may be a great fit for you. This field is constantly evolving, with new technologies and techniques emerging all the time. As an Interactive Media Major, you'll have the opportunity to work on projects that push the boundaries of what's possible, and you'll be prepared for a variety of careers in the tech and creative industries.
